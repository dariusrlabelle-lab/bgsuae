Who We Are

"Birdies are unicorns. Three-putts are tradition."

Bogey Golf Society is a wildly confident, proudly inclusive crew who treat birdies like unicorns and three-putts like tradition. We play for vibes, questionable swing advice, and just enough "competition" to justify monthly medals, Opens throughout the year, and annual trips that become lore.


Come for the golf… stay for the banter, the camaraderie, and the perfectly unnecessary seriousness we bring to a very unnecessary game.

The Philosophy "Just enough competition to justify a post-round debrief."
